ABOUT ME

I am a web developer with experience in Ruby on Rails, Javascript, and React.js. Education and entrepreneurship were my first introductions to software. As a Teach For America corps member, I leveraged education apps daily to provide personalized and differentiated instruction. As the fifth employee of an edtech startup, I scaled the operations from an idea to a SaaS platform hosting thousands of executives from over a hundred Fortune 500 companies.

Not only do I have the analytical, problem-solving chops to squash the most nasty bugs, but I also have a deep empathy for the user experience and keep that top of mind with every line of code I write.

I love collaborating with others to build beautiful products that empower others to lead happier and more productive lives.

Findaloo
